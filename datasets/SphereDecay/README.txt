CFD and WEC-SIM data for the OES Task 10 Sphere Decay test
Sphere diameter 300 mm 
Files are named as 0Xwhatever to denote 0.X diameter drop height

The columns of the CFD files are:
time position(surge sway heave pitch roll yaw) velocity(6 DoF) pressure-force/moment(6 DoF) viscous-force/moment(6DoF)

The columns of the Moody output are: 
time position(surge sway heave pitch roll yaw) velocity(6 DoF) excitation-force(6 DoF) radiation-force(6DoF) added-mass-force(6DoF) restoring-force(6DoF) morrison-force(6DoF) linear-damping-force(6DoF)

The morrison force and the linear-damping force are 0 in the present formulation.
